[DEV]

Game by Lulu, written in FreePascal with the awesome Lazarus IDE.

The following libraries are used for its development:
- OGLCScene to render graphics with OpenGL  https://github.com/Lulu04/OGLCScene
- ALSound to render sounds and musics  https://github.com/Lulu04/ALSound
- BGRABitmap to handle fonts, images and svg files.
